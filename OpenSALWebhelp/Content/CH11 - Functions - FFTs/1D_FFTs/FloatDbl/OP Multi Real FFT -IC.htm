Description

The fftm_ropx(), fftm_ropdx(), fftm_roptx(), and fftm_roptdx() functions are multiple (batch) versions of the fft_ropx(), fft_roptx(), fft_ropdx(), and fft_roptdx() functions. Use these functions to efficiently process small FFTs (less than 512 points). These functions work for any FFT of 4 points or larger.
