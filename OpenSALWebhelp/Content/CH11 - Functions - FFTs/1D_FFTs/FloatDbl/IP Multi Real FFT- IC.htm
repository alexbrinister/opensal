Description

The fftm_ripx(), fftm_ripdx(), fftm_riptx(), and fftm_riptdx() functions are multiple (batch) versions of the fft_ripx(), fft_ripdx(), fft_riptx(), and fft_riptdx() functions. Use these functions to efficiently process small FFTs (less than 512 points). These functions work for any FFT of 4 points or larger.
