Description

The fftm_zripx(), fftm_zripdx(), fftm_zriptx(), and fftm_zriptdx() functions are multiple (batch) versions of the fft_zripx(), fft_zriptx(), fft_zripdx(), and fft_zriptdx() functions. Use these functions to efficiently process small FFTs (less than 512 points). These functions work for any complex FFT of 4 points or larger, or for any real FFT of 8 points or larger.

Real data is stored in split complex form, with odd reals stored on the imaginary side of the split complex form and even reals in stored on the real side.
