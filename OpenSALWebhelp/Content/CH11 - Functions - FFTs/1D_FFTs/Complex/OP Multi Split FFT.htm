Description

The fftm_zopx(), fftm_zopdx(), fftm_zoptx(), and fftm_zoptdx() functions are multiple (batch) versions of the fft_zopx(), fft_zoptx(), fft_zopdx(), and fft_zoptdx() functions. Use these functions to efficiently process small FFTs (less than 512 points). These functions work for any FFT of 4 points or larger.
