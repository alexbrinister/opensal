Returning Items for Warranty Repair

To return (Mercury-sold) equipment to Mercury, a Return Material Authorization Number (RMA) is required. You can request an RMA at https://customers.mc.com/contact/rma_request.cfm.

RMAs are provided the next business day from the receipt of complete RMA request information. Return shipment instructions are provided with the RMA.

For hardware RMA support, select a repair cycle time that makes the most sense for your business. Mercury offers advanced RMA and on-site spares capabilities. Mercury assumes all shipping costs from the factory to the customer for repair and replacement.

Important 
You are responsible for paying shipping expenses from your location to the factory. You are also responsible for insuring returned products, and you assume the risk of loss during shipment to Mercury. Collect-on-delivery (C.O.D.) packages are not accepted.

Whether for repair or credit, prepare all items you are returning in the same manner.

To return a Mercury Product:

  1. Contact Mercury to obtain a Return Material Authorization (RMA) number.

    An RMA number is provided within one business day from the receipt of complete RMA request information. Return shipment instructions are provided with the RMA number.

  2. Pack the item you are returning in the original (or equivalent) packing materials. Mercury offers replacement packing materials.
  3. Include a copy of the invoice and a letter describing the reason for the return. Make sure to indicate any tests you have run and any error messages reported by system diagnostics.
  4. If the return is for credit, include any accessories that belong with the item, such as:
  5. Power cables
  6. Media such as CDs
  7. Documentation (user guides)
  8. Write the RMA number clearly and prominently on the outside of the shipping container.

Important 
Returns that are missing any of the preceding requirements will be refused at the Mercury receiving dock and returned to you.
