Service and Support Programs

Mercury Computer Systems, Inc. provides a full complement of product support and customer services to ensure that you have the best possible experience with your product.

Support Programs

Mercury provides the following support programs.

Online Services

Mercury’s comprehensive online services include online support tools, technical manuals and publications, and training registration. To access these services, log on to https://customers.mc.com/.

To log a support request online, you must have an online ID. If you do not have an online ID, you can request one at the customer Web site.

Tiered Service Plans

Mercury’s range of support levels gives you the flexibility to choose a level of support that best suits your environment. You can select Bronze, Silver, or Gold service, depending on the product purchased. We can also work with you to design a custom support program.

Skilled Mercury Customer Support team members are available according to the terms of your support package. Mercury guarantees that customers will receive an acknowledgement of a request for technical support with a resolution or status update within the period designated by the selected level of service.

Life Cycle and Long-Term Support Services

With Mercury Computer Systems long-term maintenance, life-cycle planning, and spares and components management, you can be assured of full support throughout the life of your product or program.

Long-Term Maintenance Programs

To ensure the successful support of your products, Mercury offers long-term maintenance programs. You need not worry about obsolescence or availability of support for Mercury products. Mercury long-term maintenance programs can include:

  • Technical support (phone, email, and Web)
  • Onsite support
  • Guaranteed availability of any system or component for the life of your product or program

Full Life-Cycle Support

Mercury provides life-cycle planning programs tailored to your individual requirements for:

  • Revision management
  • Obsolescence planning and legacy product management
  • Post-retrofit systems integration and testing
  • End-of-life planning, including fixed-price repairs

Spares and Components Management

Spares procurement and management services ensure product and component availability over the long term, and take advantage of solution alternatives. Mercury team members work with you from early in the product’s life cycle to develop specific programs for:

  • Spares purchase and inventory
  • Component exchange
  • Facilities management
  • Last-time-buy product and components stocking

Service Programs

Mercury provides the following service programs.

Site Preparation Services

During site preparation, a Mercury Technical Support Engineer (TSE) works with your team to conduct a remote evaluation of your specific site requirements. Our engineers help you plan and prepare for your unique cabling, power, environmental, and space planning requirements. They assist you to ensure site readiness on product delivery.

Onsite Installation and Upgrade Services

To implement onsite installation, Mercury sends a qualified TSE to your site to unpack and install Mercury products and systems. The TSE works closely with your team to ensure that the installation process minimizes your downtime and streamlines startup of the new system. To certify that the system functions as designed, the TSE completes the on-site mechanical installation, internal Mercury system integration, and testing.

Onsite post-installation and upgrade support services are available on request. As required, a trained TSE upgrades products to factory specifications, and tests newly upgraded and configured systems to ensure optimal system function.

Interoperability Integration Services

With Mercury’s integration services, you can reduce the time it takes to get your new products and systems working. You can lower introduction costs while minimizing risks and reducing time to market.

Network Integration Services

Mercury TSEs work closely with you to develop a detailed business assessment of your network integration requirements. The TSE designs an integration solution tailored to your system infrastructure and network environment to ensure total network interoperability through the installation, configuration, and operations stages.

Network solutions are tailored to your business needs. The TSE works with you to develop the right plan to ensure a scalable, flexible configuration and seamless integration. Fully customizable processes ensure high-quality end-to-end services with minimal disruption to your ongoing operations. The TSE can also support your ongoing and future integration needs by developing detailed specifications mapped to your strategic plans.

Validation and Testing

Mercury’s validation and testing services ensure that your new Mercury products work smoothly with other third-party products. We troubleshoot before roll out, so that you are assured of a solid deployment.

Installation and Integration

We install and integrate your Mercury systems to work smoothly within your existing configuration. Our integration experts can:

  • Streamline integration processes
  • Identify potential performance and reliability issues
  • Minimize likely interoperability problems
  • Ensure consistency
  • Enforce compliance

Secure Environment Support Services

Mercury offers secure environment support services, so you need not worry about supporting Mercury products in classified programs. Our fully trained and authorized Technical Support Engineers understand and can follow required procedures and security measures. They can recognize classified information, and know how to safeguard sensitive data and materials. Our support staff is thoroughly experienced at collaborating with trained security professionals and methods.

Our trained technical support professionals can:

  • Protect and manage classified information
  • Work with site-specific labeling
  • Use stationary or transportable systems according to your specifications
  • Follow processes for system-specific threats
  • Read configuration management schema
  • Maintain audit files and audit analysis procedures
  • Implement contingency procedures
  • Handle media appropriately
  • Follow incident handling procedures

Performance-Based and Integrated Logistics Services

Customer needs are evolving from platform-centric to services-centric support. Providers must be highly efficient, multi-disciplined, and capable of managing and measuring complex processes simultaneously.

Mercury can partner with you to provide the services you need. Our highly trained technical support staff understands the PBL model and can manage to it. Because Mercury systems and components are typically long-lived, high-value, and repairable, our support team has years of experience to draw upon to support your customers’ requirements.

Mercury can assist you in developing:

  • Performance-based agreements (PBAs)
  • Total life cycle support
  • Integrated performance packages
  • Statements of objectives (SOOs)
  • Continuous reliability improvement programs

Comprehensive Failure Analysis

Comprehensive failure analysis includes evaluation of field failures and returns in an effort to determine the root cause and identify a solution. We apply a disciplined approach to identify whether a failure is caused by design deficiencies, material defects, operational overloads, abuse, misapplication, inadequate maintenance, or use beyond design life. We then provide a detailed failure analysis report.

Self-Service Maintenance Program

The Mercury self-service maintenance program offers you an easy-to-use method for maintaining your systems and replacing components with minimal downtime and maximum efficiency.

Mercury’s self-service maintenance program includes:

  • Product training
  • Self-service maintenance certification courses
  • Quick-fix scripts to get you up and running immediately
  • Customer replaceable units (CRUs) with parts and sub-system elements that you can remove and install yourself without special tools
  • Easy-to-follow instructions, diagrams, and videos
  • Spares kits for fast repairs whenever you need them
