Mercury Product Training

Mercury offers small and large group product training programs, in a classroom setting or one-on-one. Course content changes regularly to keep pace with the latest processors and software modifications. Mercury can customize the format and content of your training to meet your specific objectives.

Classes can be scheduled at:

  • A location of your choice
  • At the Mercury training facility
  • At Mercury regional sales offices

Multi-Level Training

Mercury courses are designed so that users of any level can take the class and benefit by learning what they need to know. Mercury also offers a step-by-step program that can lead to individual certification.

Advanced Training Support

As products change, Mercury’s training services keep up with the latest features and enhancements. To ensure that your staff stays current, Mercury can provide refresher courses and training certification for training collateral/material licensing to provide cost-effective sharing of the latest product information. Volume discounts are available. Pricing varies with the number of courses for a particular location and time schedule.

To view and register for upcoming courses, go to http://www.mc.com/support/training.aspx.

You can also register by sending an email to mcregistrar@mc.com.
