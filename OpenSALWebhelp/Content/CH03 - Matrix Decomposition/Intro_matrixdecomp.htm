Matrix Decompositions and Condition Numbers

Matrix decomposition means factoring a matrix into a product of two or more matrix factors, at least one of which is a triangular or a diagonal matrix. Triangular and diagonal matrices are useful as factors, because their usage simplifies the solution of a system of linear equations by forward or backward substitution.

Matrix decomposition is used in a wide range of signal processing and equation solving problems.
